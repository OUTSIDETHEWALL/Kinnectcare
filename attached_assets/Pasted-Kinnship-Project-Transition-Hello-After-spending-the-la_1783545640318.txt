Kinnship Project Transition

Hello,

After spending the last couple of days talking with you and having you audit the Kinnship codebase, I've made my decision.

I'd like Replit to take over as the primary development platform for Kinnship.

This wasn't a decision I made lightly.

Emergent helped me build Kinnship from an idea into a real application, and I'm grateful for that. But Kinnship has entered a different phase of its life. I'm no longer building features—I'm preparing a production application for public beta. That requires a different mindset, and your approach to debugging has given me confidence.

The biggest difference I've seen is your insistence on understanding the code before proposing a fix. That's exactly what I've been looking for.

Going forward, I want us to follow one simple rule:

No code changes until the suspected root cause has been explained, confidence has been stated, and there is a plan to verify both the diagnosis and the repair.

I don't expect perfection. Bugs happen. What I want is a disciplined engineering process.
